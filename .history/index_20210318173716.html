Description - Logo Mark/Type Selection
- The inspiration behind our logo is a refridgerator and a recipe book. The idea is that the fridge contains resources like a recipe book and is used like a post-it note on a fridge. The colors and design suit our target audience of 26-34 year olds.
Meaning of Shapes
-Rectangle represents stability. It is one of the most used shape in the design. Geometric shapes are symmetrical and recognizeable. They consist of straight and rational lines. The meaning of rectangles are stability, balance, and strength.
Logo Font Style
- Wendy One is a san serif font. It is inspired by the font “Stabilo”.
Key Design Principles
1. Balance - asymmetrical balance. The distribution of the rectangle is unbalance. The left side of the fridge is “bigger” than the left side which is “smaller”. It adds attention and draws your eyes to the left as it has a geometric shape within the long rectangle.
2. Line - leads the eye on where to look and directs focus. Defines, contours, and outlines the shape.
3. Repetition - repeating a single element many times. In our logo, the repeating of the rings brings a sense of unity and consistency.
4. Colour/Contrast - has light and dark colours with small and larges shapes. In our logo we use two tones of green.
5. Proximity - Rings are close together to appear as one group but when separate, they are separate objects.
