The main colours will be white, pink, and green. For a clean and minimal look, we will be using white as the background colour so the other texts, buttons, and etc will be readable. For the landing page, because it is the first page that users will land on, we will use a light pink to differentiate between landing page and the other pages. We decided to name our colour palettes based on vegetables as our application is about saving your vegetables and using what's in your fridge and using it towards the recipes. The button colours will be pink, yellow, orange, and green.
